# Origin

Boardroom Sparring Partner exists after the scorecards, board brief, diligence pack, vertical brief, governance registry, deal radar, timing signal index, partner graph, exit room, claims-vs-reality engine, and revenue scorecard.

The goal is to turn that executive-intelligence estate into one explicit board-prep and pushback-rehearsal surface:

- what the board is likely to ask first
- which claims are memo-defensible now
- where pushback is still justified
- what leadership should rehearse before the next board or investor conversation
